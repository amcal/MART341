No-Bake Oatmeal Cookies

No-bake cookies, or "quick cookies," are a simple and delicious recipe that you can make in under twenty minutes, no baking required! Only six ingredients are needed.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups quick oats
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 stick (1/2 cup) butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla (optional)

Directions

Melt butter in medium saucepan over low heat. Once dissolved, add cocoa, sugar, vanilla, and milk. Heat to a boil, stirring often. Boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in oatmeal. Drop by spoonfuls onto wax paper and allow to harden. Makes about 3 dozen cookies.

Optional: Use an 8in-by-8in pan and spread the mixture evenly around for cookie bars.
